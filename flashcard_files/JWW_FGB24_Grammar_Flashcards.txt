Which prepositions take only the dative?	ἐν (in) and σύν (with).
Which prepositions take only the accusative?	ἀνά (up) and εἰς (into).
Which prepositions take either the genitive or the accusative?	ἀμφί (about) διά (through on account of) κατά (down) μετά (in company with after) and ὑπέρ (over).
Which prepositions take either the genitive the dative or the accusative?	ἐπί (on upon) παρά (alongside of beside) περί (round about) πρός (over against at to) and ὑπό (under).
